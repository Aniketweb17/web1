How We Make It

From Pulp to Perfect Page

Every notebook begins with careful material selection and ends with a quality check. Here's our process.

01
Material Sourcing
We source eco-certified, acid-free paper from trusted mills across India and Europe, ensuring every sheet meets our weight and smoothness standards.
02
Ruling & Printing
High-speed ruling machines apply single-line, double-line, graph, or dot-grid patterns with micron-level precision on both sides of every leaf.
03
Cover & Binding
Covers are printed, UV-coated or foil-stamped, then bound via case-binding, spiral, or perfect-bind — tailored to product specification.
04
QC & Dispatch
Each batch passes a 12-point quality inspection — page count, binding strength, cover alignment, and ink density — before packing and dispatch.
